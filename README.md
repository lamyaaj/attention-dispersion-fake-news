# attention-dispersion-fake-news
A collaborative data science project evaluating the impact of AI recommendation loops on human attention dispersion and fake news propagation across Instagram, YouTube, Reddit, and Facebook. Developed advanced EDA, machine learning predictive models (86.37% accuracy), and an interactive Streamlit Dashboard.
# Analysis Quantifying the Impact of AI Personalization Platforms on Attention Dispersion and Fake News Propagation
